Artist Statement

I was always drawn to the notion of creative freedom and spent a considerable part of my childhood transforming my crazy ideas into something tangible such as creating high heels out of clear plastic cover sheets to become Cinderella or starting my own printer paper automobile shop. Art has always been my form of communication where I can share my interest with others. As I took digital art classes, I discovered that computer programs are like no other traditional mediums and instantly felt passionate using it. With digital technology, it feels empowering to see the final product after spending an exorbitant amount of time create different art works such as videos, designs, animation and more. Therefore, I’m determined that majoring in Digital Media Arts will fully utilize my vivid imagination and share it with others.
